About the Community

An exclusive collection of towns & singles at the edge of Bronte Provincial Park.

Caivan Riverview is a new freehold community in South Oakville at Bronte Rd. & Upper Middle Rd. W — five minutes to Bronte GO, five minutes to the lake, one minute to the QEW. Connected to Bronte Creek Provincial Park and 1,690 acres of lush greenery, it's built by Caivan, an industry-leading builder with a 16-year track record of over 4,200 homes delivered and zero cancelled projects. An exclusive collection of Riverview Towns (back-to-back), Advantage Townhomes, and 45', 46' and 50' single detached homes.

Community details

Caivan Riverview site plan — lot layout across Blocks 1–11 at Bronte Rd. & Upper Middle Rd. W, South Oakville.

Surrounded by Bronte Creek Provincial Park (1,690 acres of greenery). Five minutes to Bronte GO Station, five minutes to Lake Ontario Waterfront, one minute to the QEW / Hwy 403 / 407, ten minutes to Downtown Oakville.

Deposits are staggered over roughly the first year. Initial deposit must be paid by credit card or bank draft. Post-dated cheques are provided for remaining deposits. Business name: Caivan (Bronte River) Inc.

What home types are at Riverview?
Back-to-back Riverview Towns, Advantage Townhomes (dual-front with legal secondary suite), and 45', 46' and 50' single detached homes.

When is first tentative occupancy?
Spring 2027.

Does Riverview qualify for the HST rebate?
Yes. Eligible homebuyers get up to $130,000 off — the current pricelist pricing already factors the rebate in and is applicable to all homebuyers (investors too).

Can non-residents purchase?
Yes, as long as you have a 183-day work permit.

Can I purchase under a corporation?
Yes. Purchaser must be on the Agreement and can direct title on closing.

Is there a conditional period?
Yes — 10 days.

Is mortgage pre-approval required?
Yes — purchasers need to provide a satisfactory mortgage pre-approval.

What is the maintenance fee?
Townhomes: $149.99/mo · Singles: $159.99/mo. Covers landscape maintenance of parkette, street lighting, main roads snow clearing, and maintenance of common areas.
