Policy Update

HST rebate on new builds

A significant HST rebate is coming for new build construction — this does not apply to resale. Here's what we know so far.

  • Purchase price between $1M – $1.5M qualifies for up to $130,000 back.
  • Eligible for both primary residence use and investors — not limited to owner-occupants.
  • Must be purchased directly from the builder on the original builder form. If the title has ever transferred, this rebate does not apply.
  • The unit must never have been lived in — brand new and unoccupied, purchased from the builder only.

Details are still being finalized by the government. Stay tuned to this site for updates as the policy becomes clearer — we'll post everything here as soon as it's confirmed.
