About the Building

A New Pinnacle in Yorkville

11 Yorkville — known as 11YV — is a landmark luxury condominium rising 65 storeys in the heart of Toronto's most prestigious neighbourhood. Developed by Metropia, RioCan Living, and Capital Developments, and designed by acclaimed Sweeny & Co Architects, the building is a confident architectural statement that complements the heritage character of Yorkville while pushing the neighbourhood forward.


Interiors are crafted by Cecconi Simone, featuring three curated colour schemes, polished marble countertops, Miele appliance packages, integrated wine fridges, and custom millwork throughout. From its dramatic 2-storey lobby to its infinity-edge pool surrounded by fireplaces and black marble, 11YV is a building composed with rare sophistication.
