Where the CN Tower is your neighbour

Concord Canada House is the crown jewel of Concord CityPlace — Toronto’s most ambitious master-planned waterfront community. Two towers rising 68 and 79 storeys at Spadina and Bremner, steps from the CN Tower, Rogers Centre, and Lake Ontario.

This is the last two towers at CityPlace, completing a 45-acre community that has reshaped Toronto’s waterfront. Every suite offers views that were previously only available from the CN Tower observation deck itself — sweeping panoramas of Lake Ontario, the islands, and the entire city grid.

The building sets a new standard for amenity design, featuring the tallest residential amenity in Canada at the rooftop level — the Sky Lounge — along with a Sky Pool, Sky Gym, private dining, wine lounge, and over 25,000 sq ft of retail at grade.
