The Well

Front St W & Spadina Ave · Toronto, ON

The Well is Toronto's most ambitious mixed-use development — 7.8 acres at the corner of Front, Spadina and Wellington, combining 3.1 million square feet of retail, office, and residential space. Built by Tridel, Canada's leading condo developer with over 80,000 homes delivered. Two distinct series of residences: the soaring Classic Series towers along Front Street, and the intimate 14-storey Signature Series boutique building on Wellington.

Extraordinary Retail

The design of The Well incorporates numerous unique features, including significant publicly accessible areas and three new north-south pedestrian streets intersecting a glass-canopied east-west pedestrian area connecting all buildings within the development. Total retail: 500,000 sq ft.

3 Million Sq Ft

The Well offers 3 million sq ft of convenient services, food, entertainment, retail and workspace — all within one spectacular community. Residents experience the full spectrum of urban living without needing to leave.

Classic Series

Soaring above Front Street

The Classic Series consists of two towers along Front Street with 400 and 258 suites respectively. Modern high-rise living with spectacular city views, leading-edge finishes, and direct access to The Well's 500,000 sq ft of retail and amenities below.
