Features & Finishes

Exterior

  • Pixelated mountainous facade designed by Bjarke Ingels Group
  • Four distinct “mountain” peaks across the King Street frontage
  • Cascading terraces with integrated planters on every level
  • Heritage-inspired masonry base along King Street West
  • Curated retail and gallery spaces at grade

Sustainable Design

  • Targeting LEED Gold certification
  • Green roofs and biodiverse landscaping throughout
  • High-performance curtain wall glazing system
  • Energy recovery ventilation in every suite

Lobbies & Amenities

  • Grand double-height lobby with curated art installations
  • Residents’ lounge and co-working library
  • Private dining room with catering kitchen
  • Children’s play area and outdoor courtyard

Mountain Spa & Pool

  • 75-foot lap pool with floor-to-ceiling glazing
  • Hot and cold plunge pools
  • Eucalyptus steam room and dry sauna
  • State-of-the-art fitness centre with yoga studio

Suite Interiors

  • Ceiling heights up to 10 feet with floor-to-ceiling windows
  • Wide-plank European oak hardwood flooring
  • Integrated Miele appliance package
  • Natural stone countertops and backsplashes
  • Expansive private terraces with mountain-like planters
