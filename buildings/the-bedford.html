About the Building

Parisian Refinement in the Heart of Yorkville

The Bedford is an intimate boutique residence at 287 Davenport Road — just 13 storeys and 90 exceptionally large suites inspired by the timeless elegance of Parisian Haussmann architecture. Developed by Burnac Corporation and designed by Audax Architecture, The Bedford brings a rare sense of human scale and European classicism to one of Toronto's most coveted addresses.


Interiors by Studio Paolo Ferrari blend artisanal craftsmanship with contemporary luxury: rich material palettes, bespoke millwork, and generous proportions averaging 1,853 sq ft per suite. Landscape design by Ron Holbrook grounds the building in its lush midtown setting, while property management by Forest Hill Group ensures white-glove service from day one.
