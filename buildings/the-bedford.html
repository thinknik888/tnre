The Bedford

287 Davenport Road, Toronto

Inspired by the timeless elegance of Parisian Haussmann architecture, The Bedford is a 13-storey boutique residence offering only 90 suites. With architecture by Audax, interiors by Studio Paolo Ferrari, and five-star hotel-style services by Burnac, this is one of Yorkville's most distinguished new addresses.
