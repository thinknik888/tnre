A modern sanctuary rising in Liberty Village

8 Temple is a boutique mid-rise at 8 Temple Avenue in the heart of Liberty Village — one of Toronto’s most vibrant and walkable neighbourhoods. Developed by Curated Properties and Austin Birch, this intimate collection of thoughtfully designed suites offers a rare opportunity in one of the city’s most sought-after communities.

Every suite includes HST in the purchase price, with promo pricing and extended deposit structures making this one of the most accessible new developments in the city. Amenities include a rooftop terrace with kitchen and lounge, fitness centre, kids room, sauna, and beautifully designed lobby spaces.
