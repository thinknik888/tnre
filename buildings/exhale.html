Exhale Residences on Lakeshore

Boutique lakeside living directly across from Lakeshore Park. Exhale Residences sits at the border of Etobicoke and Mississauga, offering lake views to the south and golf course views to the north. Steps to Marie Curtis Park, Etobicoke Creek trails, and Long Branch GO Station. 14,585 sq ft of amenities including rooftop terraces, fitness facility, private dining room, pet spa, children’s play area, work-share lounge, and concierge services.
