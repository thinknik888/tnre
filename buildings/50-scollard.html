50 Scollard

Yorkville, Toronto

Designed by Pritzker Prize-winning architect Norman Foster, 50 Scollard is a 41-storey tower with only 64 residences — one of Toronto's most exclusive addresses. Interiors by Studio Munge, at the corner of Bay and Scollard in the heart of Yorkville.
