A landmark residence on Spadina

101 Spadina is a striking 39-storey tower rising at 101 Spadina Avenue in the heart of Toronto’s Entertainment District. Developed by Devron Developments, this landmark residence offers 371 thoughtfully designed suites with sweeping views of the city skyline, Lake Ontario, and the CN Tower. Steps from King West’s best restaurants, theatres, and nightlife, 101 Spadina puts you at the centre of everything.

Residents will enjoy world-class amenities including a rooftop terrace with panoramic views, state-of-the-art fitness centre, co-working lounge, yoga studio, entertainment suite with private dining, outdoor pool and sun deck, pet spa, and a grand double-height lobby designed for arrival.
